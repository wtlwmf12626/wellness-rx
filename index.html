Eating Principles ⌄

• 3 meals/day — no skipping, especially before a shift

• Eat every 4–5 hrs on a consistent schedule

• Stop eating 2.5–3 hrs before lying down

• One planned sweet treat mid-afternoon, not bedtime

• 80–100 oz water/day; sip steadily

• Coffee only after eating, not on empty stomach

⚠️ Never drop below 1,400 cal on any day
